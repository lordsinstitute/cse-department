About BrainScan AI

Deep Learning & ML Approaches for Brain Hemorrhage Detection

What is BrainScan AI?

BrainScan AI is an intelligent medical imaging analysis tool that uses deep learning and machine learning to detect brain hemorrhages from CT scan images. The system employs a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on brain CT images to classify scans as either Hemorrhage or Normal.

Types of Hemorrhage Detected
Subdural
Bleeding between the brain surface and its outer covering (dura). Appears as crescent-shaped bright regions.
Intracerebral
Bleeding within the brain tissue itself. Appears as bright irregular blobs within the parenchyma.
Subarachnoid
Bleeding in the space between the brain and surrounding membrane. Appears as bright streaks in sulci.
