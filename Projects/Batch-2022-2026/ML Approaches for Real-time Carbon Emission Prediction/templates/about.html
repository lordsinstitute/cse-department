About This Project

Carbon Emissions & Vehicles

Transportation accounts for approximately 29% of global greenhouse gas emissions. Vehicle CO2 emissions are measured in grams per kilometer (g/km) and depend on factors like engine size, vehicle weight, fuel type, and fuel consumption.

This application predicts the CO2 emissions of a vehicle based on its specifications, helping consumers make environmentally conscious choices when purchasing a vehicle.

Key Emission Factors
  • Regular Gasoline: 2.31 kg CO2 per litre
  • Premium Gasoline: 2.31 kg CO2 per litre
  • Diesel: 2.66 kg CO2 per litre
  • Ethanol (E85): 1.61 kg CO2 per litre
  • Hybrid: ~40% less fuel consumption
  • Electric: Zero tailpipe emissions

Pipeline

1. LabelEncoder for categorical features (Make, Class, Transmission, Fuel)

2. StandardScaler for numeric features (7 features)

3. 70-30 train-test split

4. Train 6 regressors, evaluate with R², MAE, RMSE

5. Best model (highest R²) saved for predictions

6. Confidence intervals from individual tree variance
