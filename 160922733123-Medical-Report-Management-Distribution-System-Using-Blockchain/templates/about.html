About MedChain

MedChain is a Medical Report Management and Distribution System that uses blockchain technology to ensure the security, transparency, and integrity of medical records. The system provides role-based access control for patients, doctors, and administrators, with every action recorded on an immutable blockchain ledger.

Every medical report uploaded to the system is hashed using SHA-256 and recorded on the blockchain, making any unauthorized modification instantly detectable. Patients have full control over who can access their medical data through a secure grant/revoke mechanism.

How Blockchain Works

1

Doctor uploads a medical report

2

File is hashed with SHA-256 algorithm

3

New block is created and linked to chain

4

Record becomes immutable and verifiable
